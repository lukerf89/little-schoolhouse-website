Our Homeschool Journey

Hello! We're the Freeman family from the beautiful hills of Arkansas. After spending years in traditional education—Amanda as a public school teacher and Luke working with educational technology—we made the decision to homeschool our children.

Like many of you, we faced doubts and questions: Could we really do this? Would our kids miss out? How would we manage it all? What we discovered was a vibrant, supportive community of Arkansas homeschoolers and a freedom to tailor education to our children's unique needs.

Why We Chose Homeschooling

  • Flexibility to learn at each child's pace
  • More time together as a family
  • Ability to incorporate our values and faith
  • Freedom to explore interests deeply
  • Connection with Arkansas's natural beauty and history

Now, we're passionate about helping other Arkansas families discover the joy and possibility of homeschooling. Whether you're just considering it or you're a seasoned homeschool parent, we're here to share resources, experiences, and encouragement.

Why Arkansas is Perfect for Homeschooling

Freedom to Learn

Arkansas offers tremendous flexibility for homeschool families. With straightforward requirements and supportive legislation like the LEARNS Act, you have the freedom to create an education that truly serves your children.

Natural Classroom

From the Ozark Mountains to the Delta plains, Arkansas provides endless opportunities for hands-on learning. Our state parks, historic sites, and natural wonders become extensions of your homeschool classroom.

Strong Communities

Arkansas homeschoolers are known for their warmth and willingness to support one another. Local co-ops, field trip groups, and online communities mean you're never alone on this journey.

Growing Resources

With the Arkansas LEARNS Act providing education freedom accounts, more families than ever have access to curriculum, tutoring, and enrichment activities that enhance their homeschool experience.
